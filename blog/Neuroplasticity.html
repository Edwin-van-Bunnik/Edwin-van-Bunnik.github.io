Neuroplasticity

Understanding the principles of neuroplasticity can benefit people as it opens people up to the idea that they are not pigeon holed at a certain level, and that they can learn new information, but it also goes in the other direction, if you stop learning about a topic your knowledge on that topic wanes. In theory we evolve from babies and soak up detail around us as we grow and learn, but for some reason as adults or grown ups we may limit the knowledge we absorb, and this restricts our growing of knowledge. Neuroplasticity is not only about knowledge but it can have power over our bodies to develop and heal if we have the right attitude. By just thinking about exercise this stimulates the muscles in the body that would be associated to those exercises.
