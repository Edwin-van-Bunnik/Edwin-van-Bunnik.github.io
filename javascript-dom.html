What is the difference between Margin Border and Padding

What is difference between Margin, Padding and Border,

Padding - is what creates negative space between elements within the border that they are contained, creates space internally and this negative space is not used for anything. An example of Padding is a vegetable garden adjacent to a plaing area. The Vege garden is negative space when it comes to playing as you can only play on the lawn.

Margins allow elements to be pushed away from each other, the Margin creates space outside of the border.

Border - is what can be created around a element, Borders can be solid dashed and invisible.

Describing Margins, Padding and Borders An example of a border is a fence that goes around a property. Inside the fence could be a vege garden which is a negative space for playing as you can only play on the grass area adjacent to the Vege garden. Outside of the fence could be a public walkway or Alleyway between two fences (fences are the borders of each Property)
